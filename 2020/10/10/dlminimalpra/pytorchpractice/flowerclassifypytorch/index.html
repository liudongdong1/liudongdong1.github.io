The images above were from the Kaggle’s dataset “Flowers Recognition” by Alexander. The title of each image consists its class name and index number in the dataset. This dataset contains 4242 images of flowers. The pictures are divided into five classes: daisy, tulip, rose, sunflower and dandelion. For each class there are about 800 photos. Photos are not in high resolution, 320x240 pixels.
